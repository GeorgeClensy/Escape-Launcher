Escape Launcher is a minimalist home screen launcher app with features to help with phone and social media distractions.

  Features:
  * Screen time tracking
  * Support for work apps (Android 15+)
  * Support for private space (Android 15+)
  * Colour themes
  * Fonts
  * Weather
  * Search
  * Widgets
  * Double tap to lock
  * Hide apps
  * Countdown before opening apps you choose
  * You can disable the screen time dashboard
  * Customisable alignments, positioning of items
  * Fuzzy find in search
